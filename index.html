Fall in Love with Greenwood, Indiana

"Pride and Progress"

Discover Greenwood

Greenwood Indiana has a rich small-town history that has been preserved while still moving forward with progress. A beautiful historic downtown Greenwood has many locally owned shops, boutiques, restaurants, and more. Our community center offers fun for the entire family. In the summer swim in our hometown water park, take the kids to the splash pad, enjoy a plethora of parks, or just walk down beautiful historic Downtown Greenwood. Greenwood is absolutely stunning in the Fall! There are many paths and trees that you get all the perfect “Fall Feels”. Whether you are single, want to get away with your partner, or enjoy a small town with the family, Greenwood is the city for you.
